Account Deletion

Last Updated: June 25, 2026

You can request deletion of your Ledgerly account and associated data at any time. This page explains how to submit a deletion request, what data is deleted, and what may be retained when required for legal, security, or operational reasons.

How to Request Account Deletion

To request deletion of your Ledgerly account and associated data, please email us from the email address linked to your Ledgerly account.

Email:
ledgerlysupport@gmail.com

Please include the email address associated with your Ledgerly account in your request. We may ask for additional information if needed to verify account ownership.

What Data Will Be Deleted

When your account deletion request is verified, we will delete or anonymize account data associated with your Ledgerly account, including:

  • Account profile information, such as your name and email address.
  • Expense records you created in Ledgerly.
  • Personal spending records and categories.
  • Shared group data connected to your account where deletion is technically possible.
  • Balances, settlements, notes, and recurring expense records linked to your account.
  • Cloud-synced data associated with your Ledgerly account.
Account data Expenses Groups Balances Recurring expenses

Data That May Be Retained

Some information may be retained when necessary for legal, security, fraud prevention, dispute resolution, backup recovery, or legitimate operational purposes.

We may also retain anonymized or aggregated data that can no longer reasonably identify you. Support emails related to your deletion request may be retained as a record of the request.

Processing Time

Account and associated personal data will be deleted or anonymized within 30 days after we verify your request, unless longer retention is required for legal, security, fraud prevention, or legitimate operational reasons.

Local Device Data

Some Ledgerly data may be stored locally on your device. You can remove local app data by deleting the Ledgerly app from your device or clearing the app’s storage from your device settings.
